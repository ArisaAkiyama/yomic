## Features

<div align="left">

* **Local & Remote Reading**: Read from your local storage or stream from popular sources.
* **Extension Support**: Dynamic plugin architecture to add new manga sources (e.g., MangaDex, Mangabats).
* **Smart Library**: Organize your collection with reading status, unread badges, and categories.
* **Offline Mode**: Download chapters for offline reading.
* **Webtoon & Page Modes**: Customized reading experience for different content types.
* **Modern UI**: Sleek, responsive design built with Avalonia UI.
* **VPN Bypass**: Integrated solution to bypass regional restrictions.
* **Tracking**: Sync with your favorite tracking services (Coming Soon).
* **Light & Dark Themes**: Choose the look that fits your vibe.
